About

"I don't take photos of things. I take photos of the light between them."

I'm Kolebe — a photographer, digital artist, and visual creator based in Sullivan, Missouri. My background spans digital arts, 3D work, and design, which means I approach every shoot thinking about composition, atmosphere, and story — not just exposure.

Whether I'm shooting a home that needs to sell, a couple on their biggest day, or a model pushing for their next editorial — I bring the same obsession: find the light, trust the moment, deliver something real.
